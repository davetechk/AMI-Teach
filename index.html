The Gauntlet

Three Gates.
No Exceptions.

Every teacher passes an institutional three-stage verification before accessing any student. Not one unvetted educator is ever shown to a guardian or student — this is the trust guarantee that justifies premium pricing.

01
// Stage 01
Proficiency Test

A timed, platform-generated subject proficiency exam for each registered subject. Auto-graded immediately. The score becomes the candidate's permanent public Mastery Score upon final approval.

Outcome: Pass → Stage 2 / Fail → 7-day lockout

// Stage 02
Teaching Demo

The candidate records a structured teaching sample video directly inside their restricted dashboard — no external tools. Demonstrates real classroom delivery, communication quality, and instructional skill.

Outcome: Video submitted to admin review
03
// Stage 03
Admin Interview

A live interview conducted by AMI-Teach administrators. Evaluates depth, professionalism, communication, and alignment with platform standards. On approval: Verified & Active. Mastery Score goes public.

Outcome: Approved → Verified · Active · Score live

The Mission
"Education is not a service — it is an institution. It deserves institutional-grade infrastructure."

AMI-Teach was built from a single conviction: that Nigerian students, guardians, and educators deserve better than generic tutoring marketplaces — better than unverified tutors, untraceable progress, and opaque finances.

We applied the rigour of financial technology — role-separated access, verified identities, real-time data accountability — and brought it entirely to the academic sector.

AMI-Teach is not a marketplace. It is an Education Authority — a secure academic vault for those who take excellence seriously.

Fintech-Grade Trust

Role-separated environments, Row-Level Security on every operation, and verified identity signals. No user ever accesses data that isn't theirs.

Syllabus Accountability

Every session logged against an official exam syllabus objective. Progress is measurable, reportable, and tied to the actual curriculum being tested.

Merit-Based Ecosystem

Teachers earn their public score. The Premium Specialist tier is unlocked through performance — not negotiation. The best educators command the highest rates.
